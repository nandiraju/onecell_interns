Internship Program

Build the Future of Biotech with Code

At 1cell.ai, we’re building cutting-edge tools to accelerate biomedical discovery at the single-cell level. Our internship program is designed for software engineering students and recent graduates who are passionate about creating impactful technology in a mission-driven environment.

Why Intern at 1cell.ai?

  • Hands-On Engineering Experience: Contribute to live codebases and build tools used by scientists.
  • Mentorship & Growth: Get feedback, pair program, and grow with guidance from experienced engineers.
  • Technical Development: Join team demos, code reviews, and tech talks to sharpen your skills.
  • Cross-Functional Collaboration: Work with biologists, data scientists, and designers on real problems.
  • Networking & Exposure: Build your professional network and explore the biotech industry from within.

Technologies We Use

  • AI/ML Technologies for modeling and intelligent automation
  • Full-stack Development with Next.js, Prisma, and Shadcn UI
  • Mobile Development using React Native / Expo and Nativewind
  • Cloud Infrastructure powered by AWS

Who Should Apply

We’re looking for interns who are:

  • Proactive, curious, and eager to learn
  • Comfortable in a fast-moving startup environment
  • Excited by the intersection of software and life sciences

If you’re ready to apply your engineering skills to real-world biotech challenges, we’d love to hear from you.
